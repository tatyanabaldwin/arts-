A Little About Our Classes

Our classes are not meant to make you the next greatest artist to ever live but we do want you to love the art that you create and feel proud to display it as your own. Whether you could to enjoy a nice time with friends or to have a therapeutic experience, our classes are just for you. Read below to here about all of the different mediums we teach!

  • Painting

    We use mostly arylic paint but we also have smaller classes that teach oil painting and watercolor as well!

  • Clay

    We also like to teach clay. We have classes that show you how to make foods, dishes, name plates, and more all out of clay!

  • Card Making and More

    Nothing is better than a card to loved one made by your own hands! We also offer a special class for the creation of work using more mediums like embroidery and collage work!
